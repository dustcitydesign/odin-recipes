Meal Prep Recipes for Burning Man and other Festivals

In this project for the foundations course on the odin project I would like to share a few meals that have been useful when meal prepping for a medium side food share group at Burning Man and other festivals. These recipes have been proven to be a tasty and hearty addition to a rotation of meals within my vegan meal pod. I am not 100% vegan myself, but these are low cost, healthy and keep well when vacuum sealed and stored in a cooler for at least a week. Some even develop a richer flavor from being sealed.

  • Vegan Borscht
  • Vegan Mushroom Stew
  • Buffalo Seitan Wings
